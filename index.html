DHIRAJ GURUNG

"Cooperative and Creative for Health Education and Promotion"

Dhiraj, MPH is an emerging public health professional with a strong passion and motivation for cultural humility and social justice to reduce health disparities

After graduation from the school of public health, following different career paths and gaining experiences, he co-founded Health Connection with support from MaD Accumulator Scheme. The aim of this organization is to connect and collaborate with other organizations to promote health for disease prevention in ethnic minority population living in Hong Kong.

In this position, together with his team, several programmes and workshops on health education, medical translation and promotion are organized. In this position, he also supervises the Master's students from Hong Kong University School of Public Health for the practicum.

Furthermore, he also has experience in teaching healthcare management & social care for secondary school students and training of medical interpretation and translation for adults in non-government organizations.

He has also worked with The Nethersole of Nursing at The Chinese University of Hong Kong as a part-time research assistant. There he worked to develop health promotion programmes and research on breast cancer and cervical cancer for South Asian ethnic minorities women. He also designed training programmes and manuals for the community health workers working on that project.

While he is not working, you can find him outdoors, either walking in nature or shooting photos with his smartphone. If indoors, either he is glued to reading, doodling or listening to podcasts on different topics. However, at work, he appreciates engaging and learning from people of diverse backgrounds and experiences.

If you are interested or know someone where his experience and skills can be of help, he is happy to work together to create an impact for everyone to lead a healthy life.
