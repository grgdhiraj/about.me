Hello!

I am Dhiraj Gurung, an interdependent global health advocate and a co-founder & Connector-in-Chief of Health Connection; an organization that aims to bridge the linguistic and cultural gap between healthcare professionals and ethnic minorities in Hong Kong.

Personally, I believe in the power of cross-curricular learning, technology, design, and positivity. Moreover, I am fascinated by people, places & cultures while I also love photography to make memories and tell stories.I have a strong passion, compassion and motivation for health education & promotion, interdisciplinary education and social innovation to reduce health disparities.

In the field of public health, I have involved in health screening and promotion programmes, while lately I manage and supervise the master of public health students concerning their practicums. I also have worked as a medical interpreter and translator in different healthcare settings and I have experience of serving as a trainer of medical interpretation and translation. Moreover, I have also succeeded in starting and running community projects in the fields of health communication, community integration and social justice.

Outside of the public health domain, I have worked with diverse ethnic minority students in primary and secondary schools as a teacher. I also have served as a volunteer and ambassador for different organizations where I leveraged connections, networks and resources to develop actions to achieve the mission of the organizations. I am an avid learner of more than knowledge and I believe in the power of cross-curricular learning, interdependence and positivity. I am fascinated by people, stories, and cultures while I enjoy photography, travelling, and listening to podcasts.

Currently, while I am looking for an opportunity in an organization where I can use my strengths and abilities to work together with like-minded people to create an opportunity for everyone to lead a healthy life, I am enrolled in Health Innovation & Entrepreneurship programme to learn innovation as well as entrepreneurial processes to understand and develop new ideas for creating impact & improving healthcare at Ghent University.
